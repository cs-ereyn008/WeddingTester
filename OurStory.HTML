Our Story

Joshua and Emily's love story began in college—or at least, near it. They both worked at NOVA, Emily for campus IT and Joshua at the campus bookstore. For Emily, it was love at first sight. She nicknamed him "the cute bookstore guy" long before they had even spoken. But the timing wasn’t right, and a worldwide pandemic certainly didn’t help.

When the bookstore doors finally reopened, and Emily resumed her almost-daily coffee runs, she got her chance to meet the "bookstore guy" face-to-face. Her first words to Joshua were, "How difficult can I be?"—a moment that made Joshua realize she was destined to be a handful.

During the pandemic, with masks and work windows separating them, their friendship blossomed. They exchanged funny faces, shared brief conversations, and bonded over cringey musicals. Their connection grew stronger through countless grocery store lunch “dates,” late-night snack runs, and notes scribbled on coffee cups. Six months later, Joshua finally asked Emily on an official date.

Not long after, they moved in together and filled their home with love—and quite a few furry and scaly companions. They welcomed three cats, Moose, Benny, and Moony, along with two tortoises, Raph (Joshua's first baby) and Peanut, and a leopard gecko named Casper.

After two wonderful years together, Joshua asked Emily to be his wife. Now, they can’t wait to spend the rest of their lives together, surrounded by their little zoo and the love that started with a simple smile through a window.
